description: |
  A large, long-running community of board and tabletop hobbyists in South Leeds, covering the full spectrum from gateway titles like Catan and Ticket to Ride through to weighty euros and war games. Players from every background are welcome — most of our members have built lasting friendships here.

  Members maintain their own collections, so you can either turn up empty-handed or bring something you'd like to get to the table. Both work.

  **Weekly game night**

  - **When:** every Tuesday, 7pm–11pm
  - **Where:** Bruntcliffe Working Men's Club, Morley
  - **Cost:** free to attend
  - On-site bar, parking, and a takeaway round the corner if hunger strikes

  Beyond the weekly meet we host regular all-day events that draw players from across the region — often 100+ attendees — and they're worth keeping an eye out for.

  **First time?**

  No need to bring anything or sign up in advance. Just walk in on a Tuesday and we'll help you find a table. Plenty of regulars are happy to teach, and we run games of varying weights so there's usually something to suit. Enthusiasm is the only requirement.

  The best places to keep up with the club are our **Facebook group** for news and events, the **BoardGameGeek guild** to browse members' collections, or our **Discord** if you just fancy a chat.
