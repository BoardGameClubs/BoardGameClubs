description: >-
  The games club "GRACZYN" was founded in June 2017. From the beginning, we have been guests of the Krzysztof Kamil Baczyński High School in Nowa Sól. Our weekly meetings take place in this school building, in room number 7. We are not a school-run games club, but rather an informal group; in consultation with the headmistress, we open our activities to the town and its surrounding area. As a games club, we are a diverse group with players of all ages — from young to old. Our game collection includes games for children and family games with varying levels of difficulty, as well as games that require a certain familiarity with modern game mechanics. Once or twice a year we use most of the school's premises for our NOWOSOLSKIE NOCNE GROWELOVE / NEUSALZER SPIELELOVE-NIGHT. We also promote board games at other city events. The founder of our games club is also the initiator and leader of the Polish board game club community KLUBY GIER PLANSZOWYCH. The Polish counterpart to Stadt Land Spielt — SPOTKAJMY SIĘ, POGRAJMY! — has also been coordinated by the Graczyn games club since 2021.
