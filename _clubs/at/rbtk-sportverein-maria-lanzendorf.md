  We founded the Rollenspiel-, Brett-, Tabletop- und Kartenspielverein (RBTK) in 2022, based in Maria Lanzendorf, Austria. Our activities aim to promote and facilitate the playing of role-playing games, board games, tabletop games, and card games. In some game categories, we also organize competitions. In particular, we focus on realizing and supporting projects and events in the field of role-playing, board games, tabletop games, and card games, introducing young people and adults to these games and providing them with opportunities for social interaction. The majority of our members are role-players, followed by board game players. Every Sunday, we play a different role-playing system, which we rotate weekly. During the week, we hold board game sessions. Card players and tabletop gamers meet on various other days. Everyone is warmly welcome to join us.
