  Progetto ILO – APS is a social promotion association founded in 2014, organising gaming events to multiply opportunities to play and foster connections around a shared passion for board games. The association runs the DBL Program (De Bello Ludico) — long gaming sessions in well-equipped spaces — alongside educational work with schools and libraries. GGA (Gamer's Geek Area) days are held throughout the year at local businesses. Based in Cerveteri, the association mainly operates in the surrounding area, with DBL Gathering and GGA events taking place in Bracciano.
