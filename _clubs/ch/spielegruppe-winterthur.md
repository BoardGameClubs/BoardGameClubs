  At the Winterthur Games Club, hobby and serious gamers between the ages of 20 and 45 meet once a month. We play a wide variety of games: strategic expert games like 'Brass: Birmingham' or 'Ark Nova', more tactical games like 'Hansa Teutonica', 'Arnak' or 'Clank', clever communication games like 'Codenames' or 'Agent Undercover', or entertaining games of chance like 'King of Tokyo' or 'Terraforming Mars'. All games are medium to challenging. So, if you prefer playing Brandy Dog, want to play Jass (a Swiss card game), or are looking for tabletop games, this isn't the place for you. We meet on Thursday evenings at 7 pm, usually the last Thursday of the month.
